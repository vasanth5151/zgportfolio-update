About Zerogravity Photography

Founded in late 2012 by two visionary creatives, Ajay Ben and Vikas Alagarsamy, Zero Gravity Photography was born from a shared passion for authentic storytelling through the lens. With a dream of turning creativity into a meaningful career, they set out to capture life’s most unforgettable moments in their rawest, most beautiful form. Believing that the best stories are told without words, they found weddings to be the perfect canvas—where every emotion, glance, and celebration becomes a timeless visual narrative.
