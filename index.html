Premium Services Across Diverse Verticals — Tailored to Perfection.

At Zero Gravity Group, our services span across artistry, technology, design, and lifestyle. Each brand is a specialized force backed by passion, expertise, and a commitment to creating extraordinary value.

Zerogravity Photography

Our wedding photographers think that taking pictures shouldn’t take up much of your wedding day. Your wedding day should be the main subject of your photos. India’s leading wedding and lifestyle photography studio. Our team captures timeless moments with cinematic flair and emotional depth.

Vermilion Decors

Transform your special day into a visual masterpiece. We design wedding venues with luxurious elements, cultural richness, and contemporary elegance to reflect your unique love story.

Divine Caterers

Divine Caterers is well-established as one of the best catering services in Chennai, as we have been catering to the best in and around Tamilnadu. We are a team of catering services led by a well-accomplished chef.

Pink Ribbon Shop

At Pink Ribbon Shop, we believe every gift should tell a story — of love, joy, and celebration. Specializing in handcrafted bridal accessories, wedding favors, and luxury gift boxes, we bring personalization and elegance to every occasion. Whether it’s your big day, a bridesmaid surprise, or a curated celebration hamper, our offerings are made to impress and express.

Zerogravity Digital

Zero Gravity Digital is a results-driven digital marketing agency dedicated to building iconic brands and driving measurable impact. We blend strategy, creativity, and analytics to craft compelling campaigns across social media, search, and digital platforms. Whether you're a startup or an established business, we help amplify your voice and scale your digital presence.

Zerogravity Technologies

At Zero Gravity Technologies, we engineer intelligent software that solves real-world business problems. Our services span full-stack web development, mobile app creation, and enterprise tech consulting. We specialize in building scalable, secure, and user-friendly digital infrastructures tailored to your business goals.

Alpha Design

Alpha Designs is where aesthetics meet strategy to shape powerful brand identities. From modern logos and impactful brand kits to seamless UI/UX design, our work combines form and function with precision. We also design innovative packaging that enhances shelf presence and connects emotionally with consumers.

Alpha Packaging

Alpha Packaging delivers high-quality, customizable packaging solutions built with sustainability at the core. We help brands create memorable unboxing experiences through modern, functional, and eco-friendly packaging designs. Whether it’s retail, D2C, or FMCG — we craft packaging that protects, promotes, and performs.
