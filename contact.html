Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common inquiries below

What services does Zero Gravity Group offer?

We offer a comprehensive suite of creative services including photography, videography, digital marketing, web development, and branding solutions. Our team specializes in creating cohesive visual narratives across all platforms.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple photo shoots may take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive branding packages typically require 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What are your pricing structures?

We offer both project-based and retainer pricing models. Costs depend on the services required, project complexity, and deliverables. All projects begin with a free consultation where we'll discuss your budget and provide transparent pricing.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We've successfully completed projects for clients in 12 countries. While we're based in Chennai, we frequently travel for assignments and have streamlined remote collaboration processes for international clients.

What makes Zero Gravity different from other agencies?

Our unique blend of technical expertise and artistic vision sets us apart. We combine cutting-edge technology with cinematic storytelling techniques, and our full-service approach means you get consistent quality across all deliverables from a single trusted partner.

Still have questions? Contact us directly and we'll respond within 24 hours.
